Pinklay is an Indian home decor brand that caters to the house proud in all of us. Our designs are rooted in colour that is contemporary, tasteful, and authentic, and crafted to convey stories inspired from natural elements, diverse crafts, and cultures. We work across a variety of art forms – such as hand block print, kantha, dabu, bagru, ajrakh, kalamkari, ikat, embroidery, patchwork, metal etching, wood carving, and hand painting – often fusing traditional and modern design ideas in unexpected ways. Our collections push design boundaries and offer a wide range of stylish home décor, accents, furnishings, and accessories for your bedroom, living, dining, kitchen, and outdoors – and festive occasions, weddings, and gifts. Each Pinklay product aspires to be a conversation starter, determined to create memories.
